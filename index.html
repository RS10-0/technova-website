About Us

Women in Tech is dedicated to empowering young women by providing access to educational resources, mentorship programs, and skill-building workshops in STEM fields. Our goal is to close the gender gap and inspire the next generation of female leaders in technology.

Workshops

Our workshops focus on coding, robotics, data science, and leadership training. We offer hands-on sessions led by industry professionals to help participants gain real-world experience and confidence in their technical abilities.
